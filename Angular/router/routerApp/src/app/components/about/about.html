ABOUT US

Marsh McLennan is a global professional services firm headquartered in New York City, specializing in risk, strategy, and people. It operates through four major subsidiaries: Marsh, which provides insurance broking and risk management; Guy Carpenter, focused on reinsurance and capital strategies; Mercer, offering HR and investment consulting; and Oliver Wyman, a leading management consulting firm. With around 90,000 employees and annual revenues exceeding $24 billion, the company serves clients in over 130 countries, making it a powerhouse in its field.
